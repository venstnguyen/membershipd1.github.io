Overiew of the Membership Information

Residents inside and outside of Golden have the opportunity to join a community of those who want to help suppoort the enviorment by reducing food waste. While our counrty continues to rely on the convenience of landfills, landfills continue to become filled with food packaging waste. However, buisnessed in Golden have come together to help rectify this problem and wants to work with the citizen inside and outside of Golden. Everyoe can help reduce the amount of food packaging that is ending up in landfills by joining the memebrship program.

Once you join the membership program a multitude of opportunitues become available to you. The rewards vary to price reduction, sales, raffles, themes nights, and pairing with other businesses. More information on rewards and events can be found lower on the website.
